Recruitment Methodologies

Employees are the foundation on which company is build upon. They are great asset to the company but wrong selection may hamper growth.

Therefore, we, at JBK Soft consultancy, follow a systematic approach with detailed process for recruitment so that we provide you the right asset at right time--every time.

Below are the just some of the step we follow:

We understand you

We understand your culture organization structure, environment and future growth plans in order to find the right fit with your company as skill set itself.

We understand your requirement

In order to understand your requirement, we gather as much information as possible for the vacancy and then accurately map the requirement specific competency such as designation, principle responsibilities, KRAs, experience, qualification, compensation, preferred target company.

Database search

Once preferred and target companies list is agreed upon, we will use our sources and network, along with our database, and prepare the list of candidate seeking new opportunity.

Initial Candidate tailoring

Once we get the list of candidates, we will access the top caliber candidates whom we will approach for initial round of screening and share the list with the employee.

Tele Calling Candidate for Evaluation

Once the shortlisted candidates are cleared by the client, we contact the caliber candidates and discuss about the new opportunity along with their interest for it. We then evaluate the candidate on bases of parameter defined below.

We evaluate the candidate on bases of Technical, functional, culture fitness, job roles, responsibilities, soft skills and client specific requirements.

Arranging Interview

We will arrange the interview for selected candidates at your convenience, ensuring they are fully briefed about the exact nature of position and your company’s background and culture. We ensure transparent communication with both, client and candidates and also understand each one concerns to ensure long term association.

Negotiating offer

Following selection, we assist in negotiation of offer right through for completion of the placement only if required.
